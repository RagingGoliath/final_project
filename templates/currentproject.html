My current project is incredibly complex as a whole and will take a long time to bring comlpetely to fruition on my own. The game is called Ex Inferis and will eventually be an MMORPG zombie survival game which can be either free play or story driven depending upon your preference.
The biggest difference is that my game will be entirely in virtual reality. Taking zombie survival to its next level.
Ex Inferis has gone through several iterations, be that because of coding issues, model problems, or just straight PC explosion (not literal, just fried circuits etc). It started out as a solo campaign with no multiplayer initially but has progressed up to the current version which is what I shall be pursuing to the end.
Here is a brief walkthrough of a certain cabin that should be in the woods.

While working on this project I have encountered many problems. There is always more for me to learn and I will always welcome the opportunity to expand my knowledge.
The full aim for the game is an open world which covers the whole country, with other instanced open worlds to cover other countries throughout the world and have their own currency formats available to give further depth. The whole project is very... hopeful. In the end I will continue to work on it and push it through all its problems until I have created something very useful. If I can make even a few people happy with my game I will consider it a great success.
