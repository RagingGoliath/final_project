The First One

My first ever game was a horde based survival game. Sadly this game did not quite reach the zenith of its potential, this was due to an unfortunate issue with the AI system I was using which would impact the performance in a very large way.
I did set up the entire game, including a leaderboard and high score system, to be a competetive game, one that friends could happily play to beat each other's scores. A co-operative mode was planned but the spawn issues put the game on the backburner.

The Sellout One

My second attempt at a game was, unfortunately, based on the popular genre at the time.
I started working on a battle royale style game, which I named Royale With Cheese as a working title. I intended this game to be more heavily inspired by the classic Japanese movie, Battle Royale. This however became more and more difficult as I realised I was out of my depths with such a chaotic gameplay style. I placed this one on the backburner but with a very big intent to revisit the concept once I have more experience and skills as it could be a truly fun game.

The "Beaten to the punch" One

After learning some basics in 3D modelling I decided it would be a good idea to remake one of my childhood favourites as it appeared to have been forgotten to time. The remake in question being Wonderboy: The Dragon's Trap. I had big plans for the game and was excited to start it. Sadly, a short time after starting, I found out that I had been too slow on the uptake and someone had already remade the game in an endearing 2D format, complete with fully redrawn assets.
This put a stop to that project and I sadly moved on.

The "The Thing" One

I came across a bright idea at this point and decided that I would make a game loosely based on the classic movie, The Thing. While it was fun to play around with I ended up losing interest as the coding I was aiming for was just not working in the way it should. Things such as the outside temperature being sub-zero and causing injury or death if the character ignored it for too long. While this idea hasn't been entirely abandoned, it has been put further back in the queue while I attempt something a little easier in the meantime. The game was based in the antarctic.
