About Me

I always struggle with this one. I'm not the best at talking about myself!

Ever since I was a young boy, way back in the 80s, I had a dream to become a game designer and developer. But as you can guess, by both the time period and my age, this was a dream I would struggle to realise for a long time.
I pursued IT as both a hobby and as an education, for which I found myself mocked and ostracised by many people. This was not aided by my love of video games as gamers were rather unpopular in that day and age.
As my life progressed I managed to cobble together several IT based qualifications (and am still collecting them to this day) in order to one day try to realise my childhood dream. Then I came across a development engine known as Unity. Finally my prayers had been answered and I could begin my long, arduous, journey into becoming a games developer. Since that discovery I have tried my hand at a few Projects and learned multiple skills to aid me in my quest.
it has been a struggle, but I am incredibly stubborn about this dream and refuse to let it die.
