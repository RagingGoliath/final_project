Danger Hurley Games is an indie development studio, one which I have been working on for a very long time. My aim is to create video games with in-depth and entrancing storylines. While I am working on a very over used topic (being zombie survival horror), I am making plans and ideas for games within other genres.

The above image is the company logo and mascot. He is lovingly referred to as the "Creepy Butler" and while he seems harmless and irrelevant, he does have roles to play. Be it a simple task such as bringing the tea or being a damsel in distress. You will become acquainted with him and come to love him as much as we do.
